Our Story

Mamba Hoopers Club is more than just a basketball team — we are a dedicated to empowering youth, building character, and redefining the game on and off the court. Our club provides a platform for players to grow in skill, discipline, and purpose. We compete in the Nairobi County League and actively participate in community-based tournaments, creating opportunities for young athletes to showcase their talent and pursue their dreams. Through hard work, unity, and commitment, we’re building a culture where excellence is the standard, not the exception. At Mamba, basketball is our heartbeat but leadership, resilience, and impact are our legacy. Join the movement. We are Mamba Nation.
